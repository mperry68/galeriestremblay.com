About Galerie Tremblay

Galerie Tremblay is a premier contemporary art gallery located in the heart of Beaconsfield, Quebec. We are dedicated to showcasing exceptional works from established and emerging artists, creating a space where art enthusiasts, collectors, and artists can connect.

Our mission is to foster appreciation for contemporary art while supporting the artistic community. We curate exhibitions that explore diverse themes and perspectives, offering visitors a rich and engaging experience with each visit.

The gallery represents a carefully selected group of artists working across various media, from painting and sculpture to photography and mixed media installations. Each artist brings a unique voice and perspective to the contemporary art landscape.

We invite you to visit our gallery and experience the exceptional works we have to offer. Our space is designed to provide an intimate and contemplative environment where art can be appreciated in its full expression.
