Join Our Gallery

We are always looking for talented artists whose work aligns with our vision of contemporary excellence. If you create original works in any medium—painting, sculpture, photography, mixed media, ceramics, textiles, or beyond—we would love to hear from you.

Our gallery represents artists at various stages of their careers, from emerging talents to established masters. What matters most to us is the quality, originality, and emotional resonance of your work.

We provide a supportive environment where artists can grow, connect with collectors, and share their artistic vision with a discerning audience.

Organize an Exhibition

Are you an artist or curator interested in organizing an exhibition at Galerie Tremblay? We welcome proposals for solo shows, group exhibitions, and thematic collections that showcase exceptional contemporary art.

Our gallery space provides an intimate and contemplative environment perfect for showcasing works of all scales and media. We work closely with artists and curators to create meaningful exhibitions that engage and inspire.

Whether you're planning a solo debut, a group show, or a curated collection, we're here to help bring your vision to life.
