Cobie Cruz

Figurative Artist

Cobie Cruz creates figurative works with a modern perspective on tradition, blending classical techniques with contemporary vision and social commentary. Her paintings explore themes of human connection, urban life, and the intersection of personal and collective experience.

Through her distinctive style, Cruz captures moments of everyday life with emotional depth and technical precision, inviting viewers to reflect on the narratives embedded in her compositions.
